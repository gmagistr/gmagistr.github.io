This is me presenting at Speech Prosody 2022!

I am currently a PhD student at Ghent University under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Crocco and Prof. Dr. Breitbarth, working on experimental methods in prosody to investigate syntactic change. I also work under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Rathcke (University of Konstanz) and Prof. Dr. Garzonio (University of Padua).

Besides my thesis research, I am interested in speech perception, statistics, machine learning, programming (software dev for linguistic analysis and web dev), and playing piano.
